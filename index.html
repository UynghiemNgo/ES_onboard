How to mark your land

Click on the map to place points around your land boundary. We'll show you how in 3 quick steps.

Click your first corner

Click anywhere on the map where a corner of your land is. A green dot will appear.

Click each corner

Keep clicking around your land's boundary. After 3 points, a green shape will form. More points = more precise.

Done! Area calculated

The area is automatically calculated. You can also import a KML file if you have one, or just drop a pin for the location.
