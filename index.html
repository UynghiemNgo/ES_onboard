How It Works

Three steps from land submission to building a project case.

1

Submit Your Land

Fill out the form with your land details — name, location, acreage, and land type. Draw your boundaries on the interactive map.

2

Verification Review

Our team reviews your submission, verifies documents, and conducts field assessment for project case eligibility.

3

Build a Project Case

Approved parcels form the basis of a project case. Track your land's contribution on the live dashboard.
