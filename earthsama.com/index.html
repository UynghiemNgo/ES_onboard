How It Works

Three steps from land submission to carbon credit eligibility.

1

Submit Your Land

Fill out the form with your land details — name, location, acreage, and land type. Draw your boundaries on the interactive map.

2

Verification Review

Our team reviews your submission, verifies documents, and conducts field assessment for carbon credit eligibility.
